Welcome to Little Italy

We are a small, family-owned & operated restaurant.

We proudly serve our community with quality, authenticity, and passion.

Quality

We use quality ingredients that are fresh and sourced from our local vendors. Our dishes are designed to showcase the freshness of each ingredient and therefore we work with an extremely high standard for the quality of all our ingredients.

authenticity

We build our menu with a combination of authentic recipes and our own creative twist. We cook the way Italians have been cooking for hundreds of years while adding our own flair. This way, we can promise consistency and authenticity while keeping our food interesting.

Passion

We are passionate about what we do and how we do it. Whether we are cooking in the kitchen or serving guests on the floor, we do it because it makes us happy. Every one of us at Little Italy believes that passion is the most important ingredient.
